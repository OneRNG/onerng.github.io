OneRNG

Open Hardware Random Number Generator

The OneRNG is a project to create a reliable and open hardware random number generator - one that can be verified by the user, one that can be trusted.

Computers and Random Numbers

Why do I need the OneRNG?

Random Numbers are essential for cryptography - for your privacy and security when communicating, when on the Internet.

Computers are inherently predictable machines, and therefore terrible at "creating" high-quality Random Numbers. You need something from the "Real World", from the quantum world of physics, to obtain useful randomness, unpredictability, entropy.

The OneRNG measures this quantum unpredictability in multiple ways, and presents the resulting data directly to your computer.

But why the OneRNG, and not these other "hardware random number generators" I can find?

Because Random Numbers are the very cornerstone of your privacy, you rely totally on your source of random data. As recent events have shown us, some immensely rich and powerful organisations have been working hard to undermine the security of every computer system on the planet, so that they can breach our privacy and spy on us all.

You have to be sure that you can trust the foundations of your computer security. With the OneRNG, you don't have to trust anyone; you can verify that the device does what we promise &ellip; and what is more, you can verify that it does NOT do anything else; the OneRNG is not spying on you, or obeying some other master.
