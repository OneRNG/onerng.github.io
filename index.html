OneRNG

Open Hardware Random Number Generator

The OneRNG is a project to create a reliable and open hardware random number generator - one that can be verified by the user, one that can be trusted.

At this early stage, the team is small, the hardware is in very early prototype and the software is basic. But we have some good ideas about how to provide a HWRNG that you can verify and trust. Initially, we are concentrating on making a decent entropy source; the actual generation of "random numbers" is deferred to your OS & software.

Current State

We have a small number of units that have been assembled and programmed by Paul in his own lab, and these have been sent out to a few testers for comment.
