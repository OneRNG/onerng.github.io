OneRNG

Hardware Random Number Generator

The OneRNG is a project to create a reliable and open hardware random number generator - one that can be verified by the user, one that can be trusted.

At this early stage, the team is small, the hardware is in very early prototype and the software is basically absent. But we have some good ideas about how to provide a HWRNG that you can verify and trust.

Over the next few weeks we'll start describing the project in more detail. There will be an email list/forum for talk, hardware designs and software source.
